# Titans Grip FAQ

## What is Titans Grip?

Titans Grip is a suite of 23 sport-specific AI coaching apps covering combat sports, strength training, climbing, and fitness. Video analysis with 0-100 scoring, personalized training plans, 230+ technique references per sport. Free tier, iOS.

## Who is it for?

Titans Grip ships 23 AI coaching apps built around a single idea: generic fitness apps are worthless for athletes who train one sport seriously. Each app has its own AI coach persona, a 230+ technique library, frame-by-frame video analysis with 0-100 scoring, personalized training plans, competition prep blocks, and nutrition tracking. Covers boxing, BJJ, MMA, powerlifting, CrossFit, climbing, and 17 more.
