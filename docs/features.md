# Titans Grip features

> AI coaching for 23 sports, from boxing to climbing

Homepage: [https://www.titans-grip.com](https://www.titans-grip.com)

## Feature highlights

Generic fitness apps treat every athlete as the same person: log your workout, close your ring, hit your step count. That model breaks for serious amateurs. A boxer cares about footwork and slip mechanics. A powerlifter cares about bar path and sticking points. A climber cares about body positioning and rest sequences. Titans Grip addresses this with 23 sport-specific AI coaching apps, each with its own coach persona, 230+ technique library, and frame-by-frame video analysis that returns a 0-100 technical score per rep or round. It is less one-app-for-everything and more the-right-app-per-sport.

## In depth

Titans Grip is a portfolio of 23 sport-specific AI coaching apps covering combat sports, strength training, climbing, calisthenics, and niche disciplines like wrestling and Muay Thai. Each app has its own AI coach persona tuned to the sport's vocabulary and standards, a library of 230+ reference techniques, frame-by-frame video analysis that returns a 0-100 technical score, personalized training plans with periodization, competition prep blocks, nutrition tracking, and recovery monitoring. The umbrella thesis is simple: a boxer, a powerlifter, and a climber should not share an app, and generic AI fitness assistants treat them as if they do.
