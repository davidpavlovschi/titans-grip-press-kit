# Overview of Titans Grip

> AI coaching for 23 sports, from boxing to climbing

Titans Grip is a portfolio of 23 sport-specific AI coaching apps covering combat sports, strength training, climbing, calisthenics, and niche disciplines like wrestling and Muay Thai. Each app has its own AI coach persona tuned to the sport's vocabulary and standards, a library of 230+ reference techniques, frame-by-frame video analysis that returns a 0-100 technical score, personalized training plans with periodization, competition prep blocks, nutrition tracking, and recovery monitoring. The umbrella thesis is simple: a boxer, a powerlifter, and a climber should not share an app, and generic AI fitness assistants treat them as if they do.
